Proper bow

Received upon summoning Yellowfinger Heysel in Farron Keep after offering a Pale Tongue to Rosaria, Mother of Rebirth. The summon sign will appear next to the tower full of slugs near the Keep Ruins bonfire.

Notes:

  • Farron Keep Perimeter boss must still be alive.

  • Giving a Pale Tongue to Rosaria, Mother of Rebirth will cause Sirris' questline to fail and you will not be able to receive the Silvercat Ring, Sunset Armor Set, Sunset Shield, Sunless Talisman, Dragonslayer's Axe, or the Mirrah Chain Set until the next NG cycle. However, she can be killed at Firelink Shrine to receive the Sunless Set and Sunless Talisman from the Shrine Handmaid if her quest has failed.

  • There is a cap of 99 Pale Tongues you can offer. If you reach this limit, you can't offer more, and, consequently, you can't gain this gesture anymore. Pale Tongues used for respecs don't count toward the 99 limit.
